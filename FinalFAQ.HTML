Frequently Asked Questions

What is SWAAD ?

SWAAD provides homemade food items to the craver who is willing to have authentic homemade food. Enjoy the meal when your hungry for homemade food and if cooking is your passion, then join us to follow your passion as Homeal Chef.

How to order homemade food from SWAAD ?

You can order Homemade Food from SWAAD through our app. You can search for which food item you want to eat. Select the item you want to order. Food will be delivered to you at the provided address at your doorsteps without fail.

What kind of food do you deliver ?

We deliver only Homemade food which includes multiple cuisines, according to the customer’s preferences. Such as South Indian, North Indian, Chinese, Bakery, Continental, and many more.

Can you deliver foods from popular restraunts near me ?

No, we do not deliver any restaurant food, we have designated chefs who cook healthy food according to the customer’s choice and we deliver them at your doorstep. .

Who will deliver my food ?

SWAAD will deliver food at your doorstep, and we provide a pickup facility by yourself to drop down delivery.

Your love and care to serve cravers with passion for cooking and hygienic delicacies .

What is SWAAD subscription ?

SWAAD offers customers with regular doorstep delivery services of healthy, tasty and customized home food at affordable prices .

How do i subscribe ?

How frequently should I make the payment for Subscription?

Weekly prepaid subscriptions are available, and accordingly payment can be made for every week. .

Do I get Same food everyday?

No, our home chefs keep everyday different menu to keep your taste buds happy. .
